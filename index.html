Sacco Saving Group

Sacco Saving Group is a group of 12 Okada riders that have come up with a proposal to start an ẹgbẹ ifowopamọ(savings group). The money saved by the riders will be invested in a Play to Earn Blockchain game that returns up to 20% of the total money invested over a single game play.

    There are 3 tiers in the savings group:
      Tier 1 - 10,000 Naira
    • Riders in Tier 1 will receive a 5% interest on their money over the period a week
      Tier 2 - 20,000 Naira
    • Riders in Tier 2 will receive a 10% interest on their money over the period of a week
      Tier 3 - 30,000 Naira
    • Riders in Tier 3 will receive a 20% interest on their money over the period of a week
